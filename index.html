POCKET DIGITAL MONSTER

The life of a pocket digital monster is tiny, brutish, and short. But with your care they can live out their lives in happiness and health.

Care Tips

  • When a pdm cries out for attention (!!), it usually needs something. Check the stats screen to see what's wrong.
  • If a pdm doesn't want to do something, it will proudly refuse. Don't take it personally.
  • A balanced diet helps keep your pdm healthy. Fruit is nutritious and filling. Candy is fun but unhealthy. There are only two foods. There are no secret foods.
  • pdms love to play Hi-Lo. This is a card game where you win by guessing whether the next card dealt will be higher or lower than the last. Aces are high. Three correct guesses in a row wins!
  • When the skull icon appears it means your pdm is ill. Give it some medicine!
  • Even the best cared for pdms will eventually die. Learn to let go. Or take pictures to remember the good times.
  • This work was built on yoctoPets, which was developed by walsh9.
